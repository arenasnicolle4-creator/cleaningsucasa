Meet the Founder
Nicol Gilbert
Owner & Founder  ·  Cleaning Su Casa

Born in Bogotá, Colombia and based in Anchorage since 2018, Nicol built Cleaning Su Casa from the ground up with nothing but a passion for clean spaces and an unwavering commitment to her clients. What started as a few homes quickly grew into one of Anchorage's most trusted cleaning services — built entirely on word of mouth, reliability, and results you can see.
