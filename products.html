Our Philosophy

Clean Homes Shouldn't
Cost the Earth

From day one, we made a commitment to use only plant-based, non-toxic, and biodegradable cleaning products. Not because it's trendy — because it's the right thing to do for the families, pets, and spaces we care for every day.

Harsh chemicals like bleach, ammonia, and synthetic fragrances might clean fast, but they leave behind residue that can irritate skin, affect air quality, and harm the environment. Our products clean just as effectively — and leave nothing behind but fresh, clean air.

Our Standards

What We'll Never
Bring Into Your Home

We screen every product we use against a strict no-list. If it contains any of the following, it doesn't make it through our door — and it won't make it through yours.

We believe the cleaning products in your home should be as safe as the food in your kitchen. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, every single visit.

⛔
No Bleach or Chlorine
Corrosive and harmful to respiratory health — we leave it on the shelf.
⛔
No Ammonia
Toxic fumes and skin irritation — not welcome in a home we care for.
⛔
No Synthetic Fragrance
Often a cocktail of undisclosed chemicals. We use naturally scented alternatives only.
⛔
No Phosphates or Parabens
Environmental pollutants that disrupt aquatic ecosystems and hormone systems.
