Residential Cleaner

📍Location: Greater Anchorage Area
⏱️Type: Part-time / Full-time
📅Schedule: Flexible, weekends available
🏢Company: Cleaning Su Casa

About the Role

As a cleaner at Cleaning Su Casa, you'll be responsible for ensuring our clients' homes are spotless and welcoming. You'll be part of a supportive team that takes quality seriously — and celebrates the people who deliver it.

Your Responsibilities

  • Vacuuming and mopping floors throughout the home
  • Cleaning and sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens
  • Changing bed linens and making beds
  • Performing deep cleaning tasks as needed
  • Dusting and polishing furniture and surfaces
  • Emptying trash bins and replacing liners

What We're Looking For

  • Previous cleaning experience preferred — but not required, we train
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Excellent time management and reliability
  • Reliable personal transportation
  • Flexibility to work various hours including weekends

What We Offer

Competitive Pay Flexible Scheduling Comprehensive Training Growth Opportunities Supportive Team Culture
